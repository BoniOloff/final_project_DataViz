Exploration of COVID19 Economic Impact on Children

GitHub Repo, GitHub Page, Data Source: CountyHealthRanking.org

During COVID19 Pandemic (2019 to 2021) around 1495 counties successfully decrease the Children on Poverty.
While only 964 counties getting worse.

During 2019, there are 285 counties has more than 35% of Children on Poverty.

In 2020, there are 210 counties that consistent with more than 35%.
And 51 more counties enter the list.
No East Coast counties has > 35% Children on Poverty in 2020.

In 2021 only 161 counties that consistent with >35% Children on Poverty.
Almost half of the counties successfully reduce the poverty from >35% in the COVID19 Era.
